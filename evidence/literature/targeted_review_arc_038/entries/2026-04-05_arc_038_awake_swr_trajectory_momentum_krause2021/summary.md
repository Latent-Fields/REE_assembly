# Summary: Krause & Drugowitsch (2021)

**Citation:** Krause EL, Drugowitsch J. "A large majority of awake hippocampal sharp-wave ripples feature spatial trajectories with momentum." *Neuron* 110(4):722-733.e8. DOI: [10.1016/j.neuron.2021.11.014](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuron.2021.11.014)

## What the paper did

Applied Bayesian model comparison using state-space models to characterize spatiotemporal dynamics in awake hippocampal SWRs from freely foraging rodents. Compared trajectory properties of awake vs. sleep SWR-associated replay.

## Key findings

1. **Nearly all awake SWRs embed spatial trajectories** -- not just the subset identified by prior heuristic approaches. Prior analyses were biased by sub-selection.
2. **Awake SWR trajectories feature momentum** (inertia in velocity) that mirrors the animal's natural movement patterns.
3. **Sleep SWRs lack this momentum** -- a clear mechanistic distinction between awake and sleep replay.
4. Past conclusions about navigational planning from awake SWRs were confounded by heuristic sub-selection of "clean" events.

## Relevance to ARC-038 and MECH-092

**ARC-038 (waking consolidation mode):** The near-universal trajectory embedding during awake rest SWRs, with momentum mirroring recent movement, is consistent with ARC-038's claim that waking-quiescence replay updates map geometry from recent trajectory experience. The momentum property means replay recapitulates the geometric structure of experienced paths -- appropriate for a map-update pass.

**Distinction from goal-directed prospective sweeps:** The momentum finding indirectly supports the consolidation-vs-goal-directed distinction. Goal-directed prospective sweeps (planning ahead to a goal) would not necessarily show momentum matching past movement. However, this paper does not directly manipulate goal states to test this distinction.

**MECH-092:** Confirms that awake quiescent SWRs are a ubiquitous and spatially structured phenomenon -- supports the claim that quiescent E3 heartbeat cycles trigger hippocampal replay with geometric content appropriate for viability map consolidation.

## Limitations for REE mapping

- Does not directly test goal-directed vs. consolidation distinction (no goal manipulation)
- Rodent foraging context; transfer to V3 architecture is structural/analogical
- The momentum property is a kinematic signature, not a direct measure of map-update function
