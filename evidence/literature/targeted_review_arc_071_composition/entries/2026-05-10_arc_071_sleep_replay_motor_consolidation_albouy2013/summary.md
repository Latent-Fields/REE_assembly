# Albouy, King, Maquet, Doyon 2013 -- Hippocampus and striatum: Sleep-related motor consolidation

The load-bearing source for the safety-critical R6 verdict. Albouy et al. demonstrate joint hippocampal-striatal reactivations during sleep, with hippocampal activity leading ventral striatal replays during consolidation of motor sequence learning. The competitive interaction between the two regions during initial learning transforms into a cooperative interplay overnight, paralleling overnight performance enhancement. Sleep-replay drives motor consolidation, and the consolidation signature includes chunk-level changes -- the pattern of inter-element timing tightens, behavioural latency drops, the chunking signature crystallises.

This is the entry that weakens the slot's pre-registered MECH-094 safety assumption. The pre-registration says ARC-071 must fire ONLY from real executed sequences (hypothesis_tag=False) on the grounds that "a hallucinated chunk would be catastrophic" and that biology presumably gates the chunking write path against replay/imagined sequences. Albouy 2013, plus the broader sleep-replay-and-motor-consolidation literature it sits in, says biology does NOT cleanly gate. Sleep replay is part of the chunking machinery in biology, not gated out of it. Hippocampal-striatal coupling during sleep is reward-prediction-biased -- the brain selectively consolidates replays of high-reward sequences -- but the consolidation does happen, and it does shape the chunking circuit.

The implication for ARC-071 design is substantive. REE has two architectural options:

(a) Keep MECH-094 strict gating: ARC-071 fires ONLY from real executed sequences with hypothesis_tag=False. This is MORE CONSERVATIVE than biology. It sacrifices the sleep-replay-driven chunk consolidation that biological systems get for free, but it eliminates the hallucinated-chunk risk that biology accepts. This is the current pre-registered design; it trades realism for safety.

(b) Relax MECH-094 for ARC-071 with a sleep-mode value-conditioned exception: permit replay-driven chunking during sleep / DMN periods, but require value/reward-prediction conditioning (matching the Albouy 2013 reward-prediction bias). This is more biology-faithful but introduces residue-write through the simulation channel that MECH-094 was specifically built to prevent. The risk model: a hallucinated chunk that the value system mis-evaluates as high-reward would be promoted into the chunking pool with all the structural consequences ARC-071 implies (selectable atomically by hippocampal proposals, potentially chained recursively, etc.).

The synthesis should escalate this as a NEW REE GAP requiring explicit governance decision rather than a parameter choice the child-MECH design pass can settle privately. The decision touches MECH-094 substrate (the hypothesis_tag itself), ARC-071 (the chunking-write-path), MECH-292/293 (ghost-goal bank during waking probes -- analogous channel), and the sleep-substrate cluster (sleep_substrate_plan.md is the relevant plan-of-record). The right response may be a sleep-specific carve-out in MECH-094 that ARC-071 alone can use under value-prediction-conditioning, or it may be that REE accepts the realism gap and stays strict.

The mapping caveat is that Albouy et al. is human fMRI on motor SEQUENCE learning -- the chunking signature is correlational (BOLD pattern + behavioural improvement), not direct measurement of chunk formation. The cleaner causal evidence would be rodent striatal replay studies (Lansink 2009 hippocampal-VTA-ventral striatal replay coupling; van der Meer 2016 striatal replay) but those are on reward-association rather than chunk-formation specifically. Strong-mapping caveat: the paper supports replay-driven CONSOLIDATION but not specifically replay-driven CHUNK FORMATION; the latter requires assuming the consolidation signal includes chunk-crystallisation. The motor-sequence-task literature broadly does include chunk-formation in the consolidation outcome, so the inferential step is small but not zero.

Confidence assigned 0.78 in the WEAKENS direction. Source quality 0.80 (Hippocampus journal, well-replicated finding). Mapping fidelity 0.75 -- direct on the consolidation question; the consolidation-implies-chunk-formation step is the inferential gap. Transfer risk 0.20 -- human data, no species translation needed.

The R6 verdict the synthesis should report: biology does NOT cleanly distinguish real-executed from imagined/replayed in the chunking write path. ARC-071 implementation in REE faces a safety-vs-realism tradeoff that the pre-registration did not anticipate. Recommend escalation to governance decision rather than child-MECH-private parameter choice.
