# Rudebeck & Murray (2024) — Representational Spaces in OFC and vmPFC

## What the paper does

Rudebeck and Murray review the current understanding of representational spaces in the orbitofrontal and ventromedial prefrontal cortex across primate species. They argue that OFC/vmPFC function extends beyond simple value coding to encompass rich task-state representations. A key organizational principle is the dissociation between lateral OFC (credit assignment, linking specific stimuli to outcomes) and medial OFC/vmPFC (value-guided choice, evaluation). Both regions integrate sensory cue information with stored knowledge to guide adaptive behavior.

## Relevance to ARC-041

ARC-041 claims that the frontal cue-weighting circuit — E1 cue-indexed retrieval feeding dual pathways to E2 action biasing (MECH-151) and E3 terrain precision scaling (MECH-152) — completes the stored-to-active pathway specified in ARC-035. This maps onto the OFC/vmPFC dual-output architecture described in the review:

- **Lateral OFC → E2 action biasing (MECH-151):** Lateral OFC's credit assignment function (linking specific sensory cues to action outcomes) parallels how E1's cue-indexed context would bias E2's action affordances based on learned cue-outcome associations.
- **Medial OFC/vmPFC → E3 terrain precision scaling (MECH-152):** vmPFC's value-guided evaluation function parallels how cue-indexed context would scale E3's harm/goal terrain weights based on learned contextual value.

The review's emphasis on rich representational spaces (not just scalar value) is particularly relevant: ARC-041's ContextMemory retrieval produces a vector-valued context signal, not a single value.

## Mapping caveats

The biological dissociation between lateral and medial OFC is graded rather than sharp. ARC-041 imposes a cleaner separation than the neuroscience supports. Additionally, OFC functions include outcome monitoring, error detection, and reversal learning — all functions that ARC-041's cue-weighting framing does not capture. The claim captures one facet of a much richer biological system.

## Confidence reasoning

High-quality review from leading researchers. Supports the dual-output principle. Confidence moderated by the simplification inherent in mapping the full OFC/vmPFC functional repertoire onto ARC-041's two-pathway design.
