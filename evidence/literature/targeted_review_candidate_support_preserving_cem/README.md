Support collapse is a plausible CEM-local failure. The first repair should be default-off, proposal-side, minimal, and well instrumented. Scaffold candidates are diagnostic; support-preserving CEM is experimental; mixture proposals and categorical-first Gumbel-style sampling remain later options.
