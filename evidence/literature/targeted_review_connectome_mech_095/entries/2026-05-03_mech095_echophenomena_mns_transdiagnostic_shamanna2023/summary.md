# Shamanna et al. (2023): Echo-Phenomena Across Major Psychoses

This 2023 transdiagnostic study (N=114, half schizophrenia and half
mania) shows that echo-phenomena — echopraxia, incidental echolalia,
induced echolalia — are associated with greater motor resonance during
action observation (single-pulse TMS), poorer theory of mind
performance, higher frontal release reflexes, and greater symptom
severity, regardless of clinical diagnosis. The phenotype-rather-than-
diagnosis approach is the load-bearing methodological move: the
neurophysiological signature tracks echo-phenomena presence, not
DSM category. This is the motor-imitation analog of what Blakemore
2000 found for tactile attenuation in passivity phenomena: one
mechanism producing similar phenotypes across diagnostic categories.

For MECH-095 the entry sits squarely in the action-passivity
sub-cluster that Gallagher 2004 flagged as the comparator account's
strongest domain. Echo-phenomena are agency-attribution failure in
the motor-imitation channel: action observed by the agent is not
gated as 'someone else's action' but propagates to motor execution
as if it were self-initiated. The MNS-hyperactivity finding maps
onto MECH-095's expected output: a working comparator should
attenuate self-vs-observed-action resonance the way an intact
self-monitoring circuit attenuates self-vs-externally-produced
sensory stimulation. Hyperactive resonance + failed attenuation is
the same signature on a different modality.

The architectural caveat is that mirror neuron system is an additional
layer not directly modeled in REE V3. The cleanest reading is that
MECH-095 plays the gating role above an MNS-analog action-observation
mechanism, rather than IS the MNS. For SD-047 specifically, this means
substrate enrichment should be expected to lift the gating function;
it does not need to introduce mirror-neuron-style architecture from
scratch. If V3 substrate enrichment lifts MECH-095's competence and
echo-phenomena-analog behaviours (e.g. agent absorbing observed env
events as own action, in a multi-source context) decrease, that
supports both SD-047 and the Shamanna account simultaneously.

The transdiagnostic framing — same neurophysiology across two distinct
DSM categories — is exactly the kind of architectural-rather-than-
syndromal reading the ai-cognitive-failure-taxonomy works with.
Different surface symptoms can share substrate-level mechanism.

Confidence reasoning: solid sample size (N=114, with TMS subset
N=40), recent (2023), transdiagnostic design, multiple physiological
markers. *Asian Journal of Psychiatry* is a moderate-tier venue.
Mapping fidelity is high because echo-phenomena are a clean
operationalisation of motor-channel agency-attribution failure.
Transfer risk is moderate because MNS is an additional architectural
layer not directly in REE V3. Net confidence ~0.78.
