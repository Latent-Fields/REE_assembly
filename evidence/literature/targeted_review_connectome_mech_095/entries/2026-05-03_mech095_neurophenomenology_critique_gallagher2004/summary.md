# Gallagher (2004): Neurophenomenological Critique of the Comparator Account

This 2004 *Psychopathology* paper is the philosophical critique that
needs to sit in the lit-pull for intellectual honesty. Gallagher
argues that Frith 1992's metarepresentational self-monitoring account
of positive schizophrenia symptoms is inadequate on two grounds.
First, top-down explanations of agency loss miss the phenomenological
structure of the experience — patients do not report a metarepresentational
inference, they report an immediate experience of being acted upon.
Second, even granting that delusions of control might map onto
motor-control comparator failure (efference copy + feedback
comparison), the same model cannot explain thought insertion:
thoughts have no efference copy in the motor-control sense, so the
comparator architecture has nothing to compare against.

The diagnostic value for REE is sharper than the philosophical
language suggests. MECH-095 is built on a counterfactual_forward gap
on E2_harm_s — structurally, a motor-comparator-shaped quantity.
Action-passivity (passivity of bodily movement, made acts, somatic
passivity) plausibly maps onto MECH-095's domain. Thought-passivity
(thought insertion, withdrawal, broadcasting) does not have an
obvious motor analog: the inserted content arrives without ever
having been an emitted action that could leave an efference trace.
If MECH-095 is asked to handle the full passivity cluster, Gallagher's
critique predicts it will succeed on action-passivity and fail on
thought-passivity.

The agency_attribution_failure taxonomy entry written 2026-05-03
already absorbs this constraint by distinguishing action vs thought
passivity sub-clusters. The architectural implication is that REE
needs *two* mechanisms in this neighbourhood: MECH-095 for
action-passivity (where SD-047 substrate enrichment is the relevant
intervention) and a separate mechanism for thought-passivity
(plausibly leveraging the hypothesis tag MECH-094 and the RC loop's
pre-commit channel, since internal thought streams DO have a
provenance distinction even when they lack a motor efference copy).

The honest weakness of Gallagher's contribution is that the positive
proposal is non-computational. A neurophenomenological account of
pre-reflective self-awareness diagnoses the limits of the comparator
account but does not provide a substrate intervention REE can
implement. So the entry is *weakens* in the sense that it constrains
MECH-095's architectural scope, not in the sense that it offers a
better computational alternative.

For the SD-047 implementation specifically, this is a useful
constraint: success criteria should be limited to action-passivity-
shaped tests (Asai-style continuous self-other morph, Blakemore
tactile attenuation), not thought-passivity claims. Future substrate
work targeting thought-passivity is a separate research thread that
SD-047 should not be expected to address.
