# Gillan, Kosinski, Whelan, Phelps & Daw 2016 — a compulsivity dimension and goal-directed control

Gillan's 2021 review makes the mechanistic argument; this 2016 eLife paper is the quantitative pin under it, and it earns a separate entry because it changes one thing about how the eventual abandon claim should be framed: it shows the disengage-relevant deficit is *graded*, not a discrete lesion.

Using two large general-population samples and a two-step model-based/model-free reinforcement-learning task, the authors show that reduced *model-based* control — the degree to which action selection is re-derived from a forward model of outcomes — is selectively associated with a transdiagnostic symptom dimension of compulsive behaviour and intrusive thought, and is largely dissociable from impulsivity and other psychopathology. The deficit is a continuous parameter: how much model-based evaluation gets recruited. Turn that dial down and behaviour stops being re-derived from current goals and defaults to inflexible, cue-driven responding.

Two things follow for ARC-078's C3. First, it converges hard with Koster and Gillan 2021 on the central architectural premise: across rumination and compulsion the failure is an under-recruited re-evaluation/override computation, not an over-strong persistence drive. That triangulation — three papers, three phenotypes, one mechanistic shape — is most of why I am confident the abandon trigger needs to be an explicit, separable operation. Second, and this is the new contribution, the deficit is *dimensional*. The same under-weighted parameter produces both the thought-loop (intrusive) and the action-loop (compulsive) phenotype, scaling with symptom load. The design implication is that the REE abandon/override pathway should be conceived as a continuously-weighted recruitment of model-based re-evaluation whose *under-weighting* is itself the pathology — not a binary "has an abandon op / doesn't."

The caveat keeps me from scoring it higher. These are online general-population samples, not diagnosed patients, and the design is correlational: it establishes that a computational parameter co-varies with self-reported compulsivity, which licenses the *existence and gradedness* of a disengage-relevant control parameter — not a specific circuit and not causation. So the faithful transfer is the shape of the thing (a graded re-evaluation parameter, one mechanism behind two surface failures), and I have set the mapping caveat to say exactly that. It strengthens the necessity-and-form of the abandon pathway without handing us its implementation.
