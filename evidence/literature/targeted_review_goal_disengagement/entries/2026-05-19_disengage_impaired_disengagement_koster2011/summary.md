# Koster, De Lissnyder, Derakshan & De Raedt 2011 — the impaired disengagement hypothesis

The reframe asserts, in ARC-078's C3, that rumination is not an aberration of an inhibition-only design but its *predicted equilibrium*. That is a strong claim, and it deserves the strongest available mechanistic account of what rumination actually is. Koster and colleagues provide it, and the account is pointed in a direction that matters for us: depressive rumination is, on their hypothesis, a failure of the *disengage operation itself*.

Their argument inverts the intuitive reading. The intuitive reading is that ruminators have an over-strong drive to dwell on the self — too much goal value attached to the negative content. Koster's synthesis of the attentional-control and executive-function literature says the opposite: prolonged processing of negative self-referent material results from *impaired attentional disengagement* — the system cannot remove that material from the focus of working memory and shift attention elsewhere once it has captured processing. The deficit is in the shifting/inhibition machinery, not in the salience of the content. Rumination is a missing or weak disengage signal, not an amplified persistence drive.

This is strong support for the premise underneath ARC-078's C3 and for why the abandon path needs to exist as an *explicit, separable* operation. If the clinical failure mode is precisely the absence of a dedicated disengage operation — and the loop persists *despite* the content being recognised as unproductive, i.e. not endorsed by any higher controller — then a design that tries to get disengagement "for free" by tuning persistence or recoverability weights (the MECH-292 knobs) is reproducing the pathology, not avoiding it. The disengage trigger has to be its own thing.

The caveat is one I want to state precisely rather than wave away, because it bears on how the eventual abandon claim is scoped. The "disengagement" Koster describes is *attentional* — shifting the focus of working memory off a thought — not the revocation of a behavioural commitment to a goal. These are not the same control primitive. The honest mapping is structural, not mechanistic: what transfers is the architectural shape (a dedicated disengage operation whose *absence* is itself a distinct, characterisable pathology), and I have set mapping fidelity accordingly. REE should take from this the necessity and separability of the abandon trigger, not assume the human attentional-disengage circuit is the mechanism the abandon Q-claim should import wholesale.
