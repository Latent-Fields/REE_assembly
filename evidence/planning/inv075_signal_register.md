## Shape assessment (one paragraph)

INV-075 remediation is a **small-N program, not a 1-fix problem, but it is far closer to N=2-4 than to N=10.** The substrate's diversity stack is overwhelmingly F-*independent* by construction: MECH-313 (constant floor), MECH-314a (residue-coverage), MECH-320 (reward-rate EWMA), MECH-260 (action FIFO), MECH-295 (drive·proximity), the z_goal pathway, serotonin tone, and the conditioned-safety store all either escape the antecedent (option (i), success-decoupled) or are already in a durable-maintenance handoff (option (iii)) — they are LOW. The genuinely exposed signals are the **F-error-coupled novelty/curiosity family**: MECH-111 (firmly HIGH, and notably *outside* the Q-052 audit's scope — a finding this pass adds), and MECH-314b/c (HIGH-pending on one shared load-bearing question). The **543 gated-policy case is representative, not idiosyncratic**: it is the INV-074 *competitive* specialization of the same parent failure (a self-extinguishing establishing signal — there the diversity gradient out-competed by F-dominance — building a load-bearing structure with no lock), and the lock it is receiving (MECH-334 crystallization) is exactly the option-(ii) arm the MECH-111/314b/c signals also lack. The structural similarity means a single crystallization/handoff design pattern, once validated on the 543 lineage, is the likely template for the F-error-coupled novelty signals too — i.e. the program is N-fix in *count of signals* but plausibly **1-pattern in remediation design**, contingent on whether MECH-111/314b/c want a LOCK (share the 543 crystallization pattern) or an option-(i) re-grounding onto an F-independent novelty source (the Q-052 audit's alternative recommendation). The deferred full audit's first task is to resolve the single MECH-314b/c load-bearing binary, which moves the headline from 1 to either 1 or 3.
